Our Culture

Creativity at the Core

At D-Mak Studio, creativity is the heartbeat of everything we do. We encourage our team to think outside the box, experiment with new ideas, and bring innovative concepts to life.

Collaborative Environment

We believe that great work comes from collaboration. Our open, inclusive environment fosters teamwork across all departments, where everyone’s voice is heard, and ideas are shared freely.

Growth-Oriented Mindset

We are committed to the personal and professional growth of our team members. With regular workshops, mentorship programs, and learning opportunities, we empower our employees to achieve their full potential.

Work-Life Balance

We understand the importance of maintaining a healthy work-life balance. Our flexible working hours and supportive environment ensure that our team can excel both at work and in their personal lives.

Diversity and Inclusion

D-Mak Studio is proud to be a diverse workplace where everyone is welcomed and valued. We celebrate different perspectives and believe that diversity drives innovation.

Fun and Energetic Workplace

We believe that a fun and energetic workplace is key to fostering creativity. From casual Fridays to team-building activities, we ensure that our studio is a place where you’ll enjoy coming to work every day.

3D Artist Intern

Join Our Creative Team as a 3D Artist Intern at Dmak Studio! Are you passionate about bringing ideas to life through 3D art? Do you have an eye for detail and a creative flair that sets you apart? If so, D-Mak Studio is the perfect place for you to hone your skills and start your career in the world of 3D design!


About the Role:-

As a 3D Artist Intern at D-Mak Studio, you will have the opportunity to work closely with our experienced team of designers and animators. You'll be involved in various stages of the 3D design process, from concept creation to the final rendering. This is a hands-on internship where you’ll gain practical experience in a dynamic and supportive environment.

What You’ll Do:-

  • Collaborate with senior artists and designers to develop high-quality 3D models and animations.
  • Assist in the creation of 3D assets for various projects, including games, and promotional materials.
  • Participate in brainstorming sessions to bring fresh and innovative ideas to the table.
  • Work on real-world projects that will be showcased in your portfolio. Receive constructive feedback and mentorship to help you grow as a 3D artist.
